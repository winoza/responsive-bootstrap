About Me

Hello, World! I am currently a junior year occupational therapy (OT) student at Dominican University of California (DUC) and a student web developer at the UC Davis Coding Bootcamp. As I continue on my journey to becoming an occupational therapist and a full-stack web developer, I look forward to the day I am able to use my skills in both professions to forge new opportunities for making positive changes in the world of healthcare and mental health.


I enjoy a variety of hobbies and passions outside of school that include video games, hiking, running, reading, and journaling. I absolutely love listening to video game original soundtracks on my free time and I go hiking all around California at places such as Cataract Falls, Cascade Falls, and the Muir Woods Trail. One day, I will complete a 50-mile marathon and create my own original instrumental soundtrack.
